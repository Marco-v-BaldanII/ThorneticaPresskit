Description

Forbidden Candy Games is a Spanish independent game studio founded in 2025, dedicated to creating compelling, narrative-driven experiences like Thornetica.

CLICK HERE FOR THE THORNETICA GAME PAGE »

History

Studio Foundation (Q4 2025)

Forbidden Candy Games was officially founded in 2025 by two students from CITM (Politechnical University of Catalonia), marking the start of development for Thornetica.

Showcase at SAGA (Nov. 28 - 30, 2025)

The studio made its first public appearance, showcasing Thornetica at SAGA, Catalonia's largest professional games exhibition.

Salón del Videojuego Presentation (Dec. 6 - 7, 2025)

Thornetica was later featured and presented at the 'Salón del Videojuego', gaining further recognition and testing feedback from the local industry.
