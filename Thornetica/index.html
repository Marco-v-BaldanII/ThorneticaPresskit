Description

Meet the most colorful and eccentric members of the University of Kent's drama club! When the curtain rises, friends become rivals in tactical turn-based battles on stage. When it falls, disconnect with the gang: play pool or air hockey, meet for a walk or a date, and discover what's hidden behind these misunderstood people who have made the theater their refuge.

History

Concept Origin: The University Subject (2025)

Thornetica was first envisioned by Forbidden Candy founder Marco Baldan during a university subject. The initial core concept was to create a retro strategy RPG centered on theater, where units were actors capable of playing different roles.

Reimagining: The "RoseWars" Iteration

The project was later fundamentally reimagined and rebuilt using the Godot Game Engine under the working title "RoseWars." This iteration established much of the game's current foundation, including the main cast of characters centered around the protagonist, Gerard.

Steam Page Launch (October 27th)

The game's Steam page became publicly available under its official title, "Thornetica," marking its formal introduction to the gaming community.
